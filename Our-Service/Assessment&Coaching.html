Assessment & Coaching

Selection & Assessment

Aspire HRC houses various assessment techniques that help organizations in new employees selection as well as existing employees development. Being partners with CEB / SHL, we conduct thousands of state-of-the-art selections tools and tests. Our team of assessors is fully trained and certified. Below is a glimpse of the assessment tools we conduct at Aspire HRC :

  • Personality Profiling.
  • Online Numerical Tests.
  • Online Verbal Reasoning Tests.
  • Competency Based Interviews.
  • Technical & Mechanical Comprehension Tests.
  • Managerial Scenario Tests.
  • Detailed Assessment & Development Centers

Career Coaching & Outplacement

Organizations around the world resort to outplacement services to help recently terminated employees who are in search for employment opportunities. Aspire HRC support their clients in managing their downsizing process by providing many outsourcing services in UAE and Egypt.

Through RiseSmart partnership we are a leading provider of career transition solutions to accelerate the job search process. We support a fast transition to the next role.

For many years Aspire HRC worked closely with experts and professionals around the world to aid in finding new employment for displaced workers. Whether through career guidance or CV writing assistance, our goal is to educate job seekers on the way they need to position themselves in the right career for their potentials.

We provide the following outplacement services in Egypt and UAE:

  • One-on-one sessions
  • Career Guidance
  • Structured Coaching Interviews
  • Personality Assessment (please refer to our Selection & Assessment Section)
  • Ability Assessment ( please refer to our Selection & Assessment Section)
  • Resume Revamping
  • Developing Networks
  • Job Search Skills
